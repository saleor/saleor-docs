# Recipes

Welcome to Saleor Recipes! This section provides concise guides to help you achieve specific goals and implement common e-commerce functionalities with Saleor. Think of these as practical, focused "how-to" articles designed to get you up and running quickly.


## Available Recipes

Here's a list of our current recipes. Dive in to learn how to implement these features in your Saleor store:

*   **[Click & Collect](./click-and-collect.mdx)**: Learn how to configure Saleor to allow customers to pick up their orders in-store, a popular fulfillment option combining online convenience with physical presence.

*   **[Custom Shipping Methods](./custom-shipping.mdx)**: A guide to integrating your own shipping calculation logic or third-party shipping providers, giving you full control over shipping rates and options.

*   **[Extending the Dashboard](./extending-dashboard.mdx)**: Find out how to customize the Saleor Dashboard by adding new views, actions, or modifying existing ones to fit your specific operational needs.

*   **[Multi-Region Strategy](./multi-region.mdx)**: Discover how to set up and manage multiple regions (e.g., countries, markets) with distinct currencies, languages, shipping zones, and pricing within a single Saleor instance.

*   **[Selling Digital Products](./digital-products.mdx)**: Step-by-step instructions for configuring Saleor to sell non-physical goods like software licenses, e-books, services, or access to digital content.
