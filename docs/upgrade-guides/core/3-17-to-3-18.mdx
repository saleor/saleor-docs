---
title: Upgrading From 3.17 To 3.18
sidebar_position: 9
---

:::info
To follow the zero-downtime strategy when upgrading to 3.18, **It is recommended to first migrate to at least 3.17.2** and turn on the Celery worker to process all data migrations asynchronously.
Otherwise, you will need to downtime your solution to ensure correct data migration.
:::
